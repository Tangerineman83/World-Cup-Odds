Each card shows one unplayed fixture's modal (most likely) scoreline under both models. "Existing" is the outcome-decided-first, constrained-Poisson pick (runScenario.js's own logic, replicated exactly - see scripts/sim/compareNextMatchScores.js). "NegBin" is the highest-joint-probability scoreline with no outcome pre-decision, using current ELOa/ELOd directly. Cards with a left amber border are fixtures where the two models picked a different result (not just a different scoreline for the same result) - these are the most interesting cases to look at first. "Top 5" lists each model's 5 most likely scorelines with their probabilities, so you can see the shape of the distribution behind the single headline pick, not just the pick itself.

This reads next_match_scores.json (from scripts/sim/compareNextMatchScores.js, bundled into the same GitHub Action as runSimulationNegBin.js so both files always reflect the same calibration constants - see that script's own header for why this matters). Covers every unplayed fixture in results.json at the time it was last generated, not just group-stage matches still to come for one team.
