Δ rank = Elo rank − market rank. A large positive value means the betting/prediction market is considerably more confident in that team than the Elo model is (the market ranks them higher). A large negative value means Elo rates the team's recent results more highly than the market is currently pricing.

Data sources: eloratings.net (World.tsv) and Polymarket — World Cup Winner. Only the 48 teams qualified for the 2026 FIFA World Cup are shown. Refreshed automatically every hour. This is an independent comparison tool, not affiliated with either source.
